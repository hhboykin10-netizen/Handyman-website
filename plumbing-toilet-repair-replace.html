Toilet Repair & Replacement in Huntsville, Alabama

A malfunctioning toilet is an annoying disruption that can silently waste thousands of gallons of water per month. "Phantom flushing"—where a toilet suddenly runs for a few seconds out of nowhere—tells you that tank water is steadily draining straight past a degraded flapper valve. If your toilet rocks or leaks brown water around the floorboards when you flush, the underlying wax seal has failed, risking structural subfloor rot.

We provide professional toilet repair, total component rebuilds, and fresh high-efficiency toilet installations throughout the Huntsville local market. We reset loose anchors, clear tough blockages, and ensure every fixture functions safely without structural leaks.

Common Toilet Problems We Resolve

From simple component refreshes to brand-new, comfortable comfort-height installations:

🔊 Constant Running Noises

We install brand-new, premium fluidmaster fill valves and chloramine-resistant tank flappers to ensure silent operation.

🪵 Rocking & Wax Seal Failures

We scrape away old degraded wax rings, inspect the underlying cast iron or PVC flange, and reset the unit with secure floor anchors.

💧 Tank-to-Bowl Bolt Leaks

Corroded metal tank bolts slowly drip onto your bathroom flooring. We upgrade them to heavy-duty solid brass hardware kits.

🚽 Modern High-Efficiency Swaps

Upgrade old builder models to comfortable elongated seats, comfort-height bowls, or powerful water-saving dual-flush systems.

Meticulous Floor-Protection Safeguards

When pulling an old toilet, we drain the trap completely and handle the extraction carefully to shield your tile or vinyl flooring from dirty sewer lines. We clean the target workspace thoroughly, replace old, brittle plastic fill lines with braided stainless steel hoses, and guarantee flat-rate upfront pricing with zero surprises.
