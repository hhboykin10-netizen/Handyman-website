Ensuring Perfect Physical Bolt Clearance

If your front door doesn't align perfectly inside the jamb, a smart lock will struggle. The internal motor will bind up trying to force the bolt into a misaligned strike hole, killing the batteries in weeks or leaving the door unlocked. We take the extra time to tune your door hinges, deepen latch cutouts, and align the strike plates perfectly for effortless locking. Every setup comes with upfront, flat-rate pricing.
