Baseboard & Interior Trim Installation in Huntsville

New flooring can instantly modernize a room, but reusing old, dented baseboards or leaving gaps along the floor completely ruins the look. Standard drywall and wood framing are rarely perfectly flat, meaning standard straight board installations often reveal deep, unsightly gaps against your plaster surfaces. Achieving clean, professional lines requires custom back-coping and tight carpentry cuts.

We provide expert interior finish carpentry across the greater Huntsville area. We remove old moldings cleanly, fit crisp profile lines, map scarf joints down long open runs, and secure everything tightly with air-powered finish nailers for an exceptional, premium home finish.

Tight Copied Corners vs. Gapping Miter Cuts

Most basic home builders try to cut inside corners using simple 45-degree miter cuts, which inevitably pull apart when your home's framing naturally settles over time. We take the extra time to hand-cope our interior baseboard corners—carving away the back of the wood profile so it wraps tightly over the face of the adjacent piece. This premium method keeps your trim looking seamless all year round.
