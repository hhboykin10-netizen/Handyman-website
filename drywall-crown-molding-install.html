Crown Molding Installation in Huntsville, AL

Adding decorative crown molding is one of the most effective ways to add high-end visual character and equity to your home. However, crown molding is notoriously difficult to install properly. Because it sits at a precise angle leaning against both the wall and the ceiling, standard carpentry cuts require highly complex compound miter angles. If a house has uneven ceilings or out-of-square walls, standard miter joints will immediately twist and gape open.

We provide specialized interior finish carpentry across Huntsville, Alabama. We use precision laser measuring levels, map exact ceiling variances, and hand-cope custom corner joints so your molding stays locked flush against your drywall indefinitely.

Managing Uneven Framing and Wavy Drywall

In almost every home, ceiling lines dip and swell slightly over long runs. Standard contractors often force the wood flat, which creates visible waves or tears out the drywall. We bridge these gaps by back-planing structural high spots and using flexible caulking techniques to hide imperfections along the ceiling. This gives your trim lines a perfectly straight, crisp look.
