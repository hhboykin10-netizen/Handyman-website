Faucet Repair & Replacement in Huntsville, Alabama

A constantly dripping faucet does more than keep you up at night—it silently drives up your local utility bill and signals deteriorating internal seals. Over time, Huntsville’s hard water minerals accumulate inside faucet cartridges, tearing up delicate rubber O-rings and brass valve seats. Ignoring a minor spout leak can eventually result in a sudden structural blowout beneath your basin floorboards.

We provide comprehensive residential faucet repair, hardware style upgrades, and supply line restoration throughout the Huntsville area. We ensure every unit sits perfectly square, seals tightly against the counter deck, and operates smoothly without leaking a single drop.

Common Faucet Failure Points We Fix

Whether it is in your master bath, kitchen island, or basement utility tub, we handle all typical fixture issues:

💧 Steady Spout Drips

We swap out worn ceramic cartridges or rubber washers to completely halt continuous water loss.

🌊 Base & Handle Leaking

Water pooling around the top of your counter deck means internal seals have torn and require immediate replacement.

🗜️ Loose, Wobbling Housings

When undersink mounting nuts rust or strip out, the faucet body slips out of alignment during daily use.
