Drywall Patching & Texture Matching in Huntsville

An accidental swing of a door, a rough furniture move, or a quick plumbing repair can leave your sheetrock with ugly, jagged holes. While anyone can slap some spackle over a small dent, making a larger patch blend into the surrounding wall so it looks like it never happened requires precise feathering, proper grain sanding, and professional artistry.

The trickiest part of wall repair isn't hanging the patch—it is mimicking the wall's surface texture. Whether your Huntsville home features smooth modern walls, heavy knockdown, orange peel sprays, or dated acoustic popcorn ceilings, we carefully recreate those patterns for a seamless look.

Sheetrock & Surface Repairs We Perfect

We patch, blend, and restore structurally sound walls across your home:

🚪 Doorknob Wall Holes

We install sturdy backer wood support blocks and mesh tape to ensure your patch holds up under heavy impacts.

🌪️ Advanced Texture Blending

We use precision hopper guns and hand-trowels to perfectly replicate orange peel, knockdown, or smooth plaster patterns.

📈 Settling Wall Cracks

We clean out foundation stress fractures, apply flexible fiberglass mesh tape, and coat with high-strength compound.

💧 Water Damage Cutouts

We remove soft, stained drywall from leaking plumbing lines, treat the framing, and install brand-new, clean sheets.

Dust-Controlled Mudding & Invisible Sanding Lines

Drywall dust can quickly coat an entire room if it isn't managed properly. We isolate our working spaces with thick plastic sheeting, utilize heavy drop cloths over your furniture, and sand down joint compounds carefully to maintain a clean home environment. Once we apply your finish texture, the repaired area will be completely flat and ready for a seamless topcoat of paint.
