Common Dryer Failures We Diagnose & Fix

Reach out immediately if your residential electric or gas dryer experiences any of these standard mechanical warnings:

🔥 Drum Spins But No Heat

Typically caused by a snapped or burned-out heating element, an open high-limit safety thermostat, or a blown internal thermal fuse.

🔄 Motor Hum-Hum But Won't Tumble

Often indicates a broken heavy-duty drive belt, worn drum support rollers, a seized drum pulley, or a failing drive motor stator.

⏱️ Taking Forever to Dry Clothes

Usually points to severe blockages inside your internal vent paths or moisture sensors incorrectly tracking humidity limits.

❌ Unit Will Not Start At All

Frequently tracks back to a fractured door switch contact link, a malfunctioning start button, or an overloaded internal thermal protection switch.
