Common Stove & Oven Issues We Fix

Call or book a diagnostic visit if your cooking equipment shows any of these common warning signs:

🔥 Oven Will Not Heat

Typically caused by a burned-out bake or broil element, a broken temperature sensor, or a failed electronic control board link.

⚡ Burner Stays Completely Cold

Often points to cracked receptacle blocks, failing surface switches, or fractured internal wire links behind the control panels.

💨 Gas Burner Keeps Clicking

Usually indicates a worn spark electrode module, dirty igniter components, or moisture trapped inside the burner cap assembly.

🌡️ Temperature is Unpredictable

Frequently caused by uncalibrated thermostat links or a failing internal thermal sensor misreading heat levels.
