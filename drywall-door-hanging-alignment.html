Interior Door Hanging & Alignment in Huntsville

When interior doors drag across your carpets, fail to click shut, or constantly drift open on their own, the culprit is usually structural shifting, loose hinge screws, or warped wooden frames. Forcing a door shut day after day stresses the surrounding drywall and cracks the delicate trim paint lines around the casing.

Fixing a sagging door requires careful geometric adjustments rather than just shaving down the wood. We provide professional door re-hanging, jamb rebuilding, and alignment services across Huntsville. We balance reveal gaps, shim the frame tightly, and make sure your doors swing smoothly and lock perfectly.

Door Repairs & Installations We Handle

We correct frustrating alignment bugs and install crisp fresh slabs:

⚖️ Sagging Door Re-Alignment

We replace short factory hinge screws with heavy-duty fasteners that anchor directly into the structural wall studs.

🛠️ Pre-Hung Door Installations

We plumb, level, and shim brand-new door frames into rough openings for perfectly square, long-term performance.

🔒 Strike Plate Adjustments

If your door handle latch misses the wall strike plate hole, we shift and file the hardware for a smooth, reliable click.

🚪 Bi-Fold & Closet Track Tuning

We realign loose bedroom closet rollers, tighten bottom pin pivots, and repair damaged overhead tracks.

Fixing Core Framing Settling

As homes naturally settle into the local soil, door openings can twist out of alignment over time. Instead of just planing off the finish veneer, we address the root cause by removing the trim, resetting the underlying shims, and anchoring the jamb plumb. This restores your gaps cleanly so the door functions beautifully without rubbing. Every project comes with clear, flat-rate pricing.
