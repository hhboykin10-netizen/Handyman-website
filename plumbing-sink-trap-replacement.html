Sink Drain & P-Trap Replacement in Huntsville, AL

The plumbing pipes underneath your kitchen or bathroom sinks are highly prone to wear and tear. Over time, strong chemical drain cleaners can eat away at thin metal walls, while plastic slip-joint washers get dry and brittle. This leads to slow, hidden drips that pool under your cabinets, rotting out wood floors and creating a breeding ground for mold.

A properly installed P-trap keeps a small barrier of water inside the pipe curve. This blocks dangerous, foul-smelling sewer gases from escaping the drain and entering your living space. If your plumbing pipes look corroded, feel loose, or smell bad, it is time to install fresh, sturdy drain lines.
